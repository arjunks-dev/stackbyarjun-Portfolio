### Project Overview

Led the design and implementation of an enterprise infrastructure modernization initiative aimed at consolidating standalone VMware environments into a centralized virtualized infrastructure. The project involved migrating multiple Dell PowerEdge servers running standalone ESXi hosts to a centralized VMware vCenter environment utilizing Huawei OceanStor 2020 all-flash SSD storage connected through a high-speed 100Gb SAN network.

In parallel, an enterprise backup and recovery platform was implemented using Huawei OceanStor 5120 NFS storage with WORM (Write Once Read Many) protection integrated with Vinchin Backup & Recovery. The solution significantly improved backup performance, data protection, recovery times, and overall infrastructure resilience while maintaining business continuity throughout the migration process.
